Know More About Abisa Support

We offer excellent services
tailored to your needs.

ABISA Support are a care provider based in Leicestershire and determined to deliver the highest standard of care and assistance. We specialise in providing care to clients with mental illness, autism, mild learning disabilities, and behavioral challenges. We provide all within a comforting and secure living environment.

Our unwavering commitment is to empower our service users to think and act independently. We achieve this by adhering to the REACH standards, which are designed to unlock their full potential through our dedicated support. ABISA Support is registered with the Care Quality Commission as a service delivering care and support to individuals residing in their own homes, or their families homes within the community.
